BASIC OLED UNITARY TEST
========================

- Configure Board
- Initialize OLED

- After blue splash screen, slider switch allows to toogle OLED display between 2 modes:

> criss-cross of horizontal and vertical lines,
  random width between 0 and 4 pixels, random color
or
> mosaic of 8 blocks with 4 random colors and 4 taken from a fixed table
